# Growth Model - Knowledge Sharing Community

This document defines the comprehensive growth model that drives sustainable, scalable, and impactful growth for the Knowledge Sharing Community. The model integrates user growth, content growth, community growth, and economic growth while maintaining platform values and quality standards.
