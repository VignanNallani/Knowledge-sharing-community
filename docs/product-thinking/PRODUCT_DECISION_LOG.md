# Product Decision Log - Knowledge Sharing Community

## Overview

This document serves as the comprehensive record of all major product decisions made during the development of the Knowledge Sharing Community platform. It provides transparency into decision-making processes, rationale, outcomes, and lessons learned.

## Decision Log Structure

### Log Entry Format

#### 1. Decision Identification
**Decision ID:** Unique identifier for tracking
**Decision Date:** Date of decision
**Decision Category:** Strategic, Tactical, Operational
**Decision Maker:** Person or committee responsible
**Decision Status:** Proposed, In Progress, Completed, Cancelled

#### 2. Decision Context
**Problem Statement:** Clear problem or opportunity description
**Strategic Alignment:** How decision aligns with platform vision and goals
**Stakeholder Impact:** Stakeholders affected by decision
**Urgency Level:** Critical, High, Medium, Low

#### 3. Decision Analysis
**Options Considered:** Alternative options analyzed
**Evaluation Criteria:** Decision criteria and weighting
**Risk Assessment:** Risk analysis and mitigation
**Resource Requirements:** Resources needed for implementation

#### 4. Decision Outcome
**Final Decision:** Chosen option and rationale
**Implementation Plan:** Implementation timeline and milestones
**Expected Outcomes:** Expected results and benefits
**Success Metrics:** Success criteria and KPIs

#### 5. Post-Decision Analysis
**Actual Outcomes:**
- Actual results vs. expected outcomes
- Success metrics performance
- Stakeholder feedback
- Lessons learned

**Unexpected Consequences:**
- Unintended side effects
- Market reactions
- User feedback
- System impacts

**Future Implications:**
- Long-term implications
- Follow-up decisions
- Strategic adjustments
- Policy changes
