# User Lifecycle Flows - Knowledge Sharing Community

## Overview

This document maps the complete user journey across all lifecycle stages, from initial discovery to platform leadership. These flows guide user experience design, feature development, and engagement strategies.

## Universal User Lifecycle Framework

### Core Lifecycle Stages

#### Stage 1: Discovery (Awareness → Interest)
**Duration:** 0-7 days
**Objective:** Attract and inform potential users

**Key Activities:**
- External platform discovery (search, social media, referrals)
- Initial landing page visits
- Value proposition evaluation
- Registration consideration

**Success Metrics:**
- Visit-to-signup conversion rate
- Time on platform
- Content exploration depth
- Return visit frequency

**Critical Touchpoints:**
- Landing pages and marketing materials
- Content previews and samples
- Social proof and testimonials
- Registration barriers and benefits

#### Stage 2: Onboarding (Registration → First Value)
**Duration:** Day 1-14
**Objective:** Deliver immediate value and establish habits

**Key Activities:**
- Account creation and profile setup
- Platform orientation and feature discovery
- Initial content consumption or creation
- First community interactions

**Success Metrics:**
- Profile completion rate
- Feature adoption rate
- First engagement timing
- Day 1-7 retention rate

**Critical Touchpoints:**
- Registration flow and verification
- Welcome tutorials and guidance
- Personalization setup
- Early success experiences

#### Stage 3: Engagement (Active Use → Habit Formation)
**Duration:** Week 2-8
**Objective:** Build consistent usage patterns and community integration

**Key Activities:**
- Regular content consumption or creation
- Community participation and networking
- Platform feature exploration
- Value realization and benefit recognition

**Success Metrics:**
- Weekly active usage frequency
- Content interaction depth
- Community participation rate
- Feature utilization breadth

**Critical Touchpoints:**
- Content discovery and recommendation
- Community forums and discussions
- Progress tracking and achievements
- Social features and networking

#### Stage 4: Contribution (Value Creation → Recognition)
**Duration:** Month 2-6
**Objective:** Transition from consumer to contributor and build reputation

**Key Activities:**
- Content creation and sharing
- Community service and mentorship
- Knowledge contribution and feedback
- Reputation and influence building

**Success Metrics:**
- Content creation frequency and quality
- Community service participation
- Reputation score growth
- Peer recognition and endorsements

**Critical Touchpoints:**
- Content creation tools and guidance
- Community service opportunities
- Reputation and recognition systems
- Mentorship and collaboration features

#### Stage 5: Leadership (Influence → Governance)
**Duration:** Month 6+
**Objective:** Establish thought leadership and platform stewardship

**Key Activities:**
- Expert content creation and curation
- Community governance and moderation
- Platform improvement and innovation
- Knowledge ecosystem development

**Success Metrics:**
- Leadership role participation
- Governance contribution quality
- Innovation and improvement impact
- Community health and growth influence

**Critical Touchpoints:**
- Leadership opportunities and roles
- Governance and moderation tools
- Innovation and feedback systems
- Community development programs

#### Stage 6: Evolution (Growth → Legacy)
**Duration:** Ongoing
**Objective:** Continuous growth and knowledge preservation

**Key Activities:**
- Expertise expansion and diversification
- Knowledge transfer and mentorship
- Platform evolution and innovation
- Legacy building and succession

**Success Metrics:**
- Expertise growth and diversification
- Mentorship effectiveness
- Innovation contribution impact
- Knowledge preservation and transfer

**Critical Touchpoints:**
- Advanced learning and development
- Mentorship and knowledge transfer
- Innovation and research programs
- Legacy and succession planning

- Complex registration process
- Unclear value proposition
- Privacy concerns
- Time investment requirements

**Solutions:**
- Streamlined registration flow
- Clear benefit communication
- Trust and safety assurance
- Social registration options

- Lack of immediate value
- Community integration failure
- Feature complexity
