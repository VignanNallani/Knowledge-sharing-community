# Community Quality Metrics - Knowledge Sharing Community

## Overview

This document defines comprehensive community quality metrics that measure the health, engagement, and value creation within the Knowledge Sharing Community. These metrics ensure the community maintains high standards of interaction, collaboration, and knowledge exchange.

## Community Quality Framework

### Primary Quality Metrics

#### 1. Community Quality Index (CQI)

**Definition:** Composite measure of overall community quality across all interaction and collaboration dimensions.

**Formula:**
```
CQI = (Engagement_Quality × 0.3) + (Interaction_Quality × 0.25) + 
       (Knowledge_Quality × 0.2) + (Social_Quality × 0.15) + 
       (Governance_Quality × 0.1)
```

**Component Breakdown:**

**Engagement Quality (30%):**
- Depth and meaningfulness of participation
- Quality of user contributions
- Consistency of engagement
- Value creation through engagement

**Interaction Quality (25%):**
- Respectfulness and civility
- Constructiveness of discussions
- Collaboration effectiveness
- Communication quality

**Knowledge Quality (20%):**
- Accuracy and reliability of shared knowledge
- Educational value of content
- Innovation and creativity
- Knowledge application and impact

**Social Quality (15%):**
- Community cohesion and belonging
- Support and mentorship
- Diversity and inclusivity
- Social capital development

**Governance Quality (10%):**
- Fairness and transparency
- Effective moderation
- Community standards adherence
- Self-governance capability

#### 2. Collaboration Effectiveness Score (CES)

**Definition:** Measurement of collaborative quality and teamwork effectiveness.

**Formula:**
```
CES = (Team_Coordination × 0.3) + (Knowledge_Sharing × 0.25) + 
       (Problem_Solving × 0.2) + (Innovation_Creation × 0.15) + 
       (Relationship_Building × 0.1)
```

**Collaboration Components:**
- Team coordination and synchronization
- Knowledge sharing effectiveness
- Collective problem-solving
- Collaborative innovation
- Relationship and trust building

#### 3. Social Capital Index (SCI)

**Definition:** Assessment of social capital development and community network value.

**Formula:**
```
SCI = (Network_Density × 0.3) + (Trust_Levels × 0.25) + 
       (Social_Support × 0.2) + (Shared_Values × 0.15) + 
       (Collective_Efficacy × 0.1)
```

**Social Capital Components:**
- Network connection density
- Trust and reciprocity levels
- Social support systems
- Shared values and norms
- Collective efficacy and action
