# Incentive Design - Knowledge Sharing Community

## Overview

This document defines comprehensive incentive design mechanisms that drive desired behaviors, foster engagement, and create sustainable value creation within the Knowledge Sharing Community. The design balances intrinsic and extrinsic motivators while aligning individual and platform goals.

## Incentive Design Framework

### Core Design Principles

#### 1. Behavioral Economics Foundation
**Rational Choice Theory:** Users make decisions based on perceived costs and benefits
**Prospect Theory:** Loss aversion and reference points influence behavior
**Nudge Theory:** Small design changes can significantly impact behavior
**Game Theory:** Strategic interactions and optimal strategies

#### 2. Motivation Psychology
**Intrinsic Motivation:** Internal drives for autonomy, mastery, and purpose
**Extrinsic Motivation:** External rewards, recognition, and tangible benefits
**Self-Determination Theory:** Competence, autonomy, and relatedness needs
**Expectancy Theory:** Effort → Performance → Outcome expectations

#### 3. Systems Thinking Approach
**Feedback Loops:** Reinforcing and balancing loops drive behavior
**Network Effects:** Incentives compound through user interactions
**Emergent Behaviors:** Complex patterns arise from simple rules
**Adaptive Systems:** Incentives evolve with user behavior
